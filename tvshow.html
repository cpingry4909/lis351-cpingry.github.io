Synopsis

The show follows Marty Byrd, a financial advisor from Chicago who has been laundering money for one of the largest Mexican drug cartels for years. His reality comes crashing down when it is discovered that his partner siphones and stole from them. In order to stay alive, Marty agrees to move his family out to the Lake of the Ozarks in Missouri to continue laundering money to repay what his partner stole.
