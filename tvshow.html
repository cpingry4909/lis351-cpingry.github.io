Synopsis

The show follows Marty Byrd, a financial advisor from Chicago who has been laundering money for one of the largest Mexican drug cartels for years. His reality comes crashing down when it is discovered that his partner had been siphoning money from their operation. In order to stay alive, Marty agrees to move his family out to the Lake of the Ozarks in Missouri to continue laundering money to repay what his partner stole.

My Favorite Aspects

  • At the beginning of each episode, an O is displayed on the screen and divided into 4 corners, each containing an object or scene. Viewers can find each of these things throughout the episodeas they watch.
  • The acting is done very well, but the character development from the start to current season for some of them is amazing as you learn more about the story.
