My Favorite Things to Do

Video Games
I play many different types of games with my friends using a trusty game laptop I got for graduating high school. My favorite games include Elden Ring (this game is so cool, link included), Valorant, most Legend of Zelda titles, and Destiny 2.
Physical Activity
I enjoy most forms of physical activity and my habits have changed over the years. Up through high school I played sports such as tennis, track/running, baseball, and even lacrosse for a bit. Now I typically go to the gym around 5 days per week with friends or my roommates.
Traveling
This is more of a passion than a hobby, but I really enjoy travel and seeing new new places when I am given the opportunity. Living among new cultures is probably one of the most exciting parts of life for me. I have been to Mexico, Canada, Europe, and South Korea and hope to expand the list soon.
